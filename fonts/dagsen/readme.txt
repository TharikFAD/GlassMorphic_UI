DAGSEN is an expanded display type inspired by Pinoy classic jeepney sign type "Cubao Free" by Aaron Amar and the vintage industrial feel of "DDC Hardware" by DDC. 

Available in 3 styles - Black, Outline, & Inked. Uppercase character set A-Z, numbers, & essential punctuations.
